# Stella Was Aware

**Game 4 of 4 · ARM coprocessor (Harmony/Melody-class cartridge)**

The finale. The world turns fluid, and the structures glimpsed at the edge of the
16K world become legible: they are in a game, made as an homage. Alex resists the
revelation before making peace with it. The trio encounters wordless "echoes" —
original, unnamed silhouettes of the shapes that inspired their world — and, in
the space between frames, finds Flicker persisting: transformed, not restored.

- **Theme:** meta-awareness & transcendence — "Their existence transcended their constraints, continuing in the minds of those who played their game."
- **Scope:** 15-20 levels, 5+ characters including original echo characters
- **Tech:** C/C++ game engine on ARM, DASM for the 2600-side TIA interface
- **Homage stance:** spiritual homage, no borrowed characters — see [`docs/decisions.md`](../../docs/decisions.md) #6, including the upgrade path if Bithell Games grants a blessing

**Status:** planned.
